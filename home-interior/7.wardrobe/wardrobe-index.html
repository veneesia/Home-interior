Wardrobe Design

Vens interior design offers modern wardrobe designs of all shapes, sizes, finishes and configurations. We have a plethora of unique, inspiring and captivating models that have all been specifically designed to help accentuate your bedroom to a great degree. And nothing screams luxury as some of our high-end models that run extravagance from floor to ceiling. Besides an impeccable outer shell, our Wardrobe Units come equipped with oodles of storage space, both open and closed. And if space is not a concern for you, why not furnish your bedroom with our plush range of wardrobes with dressing tables. You can even opt for our uber stylish ‘push-to-open’ handleless drawer units that are both fun to use and add to the ultra chic look of your space. Or even our even range of sliding wardrobes that will add that extra oomph to your room.
